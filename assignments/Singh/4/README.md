## Assignment Description
This assignment is a literature review of at least five papers on the topic of **Honeynets and Cyber Deception in Critical Infrastructure**. The review includes:

- **Introduction:** Overview of the research area, motivation, and why these papers were selected.  
- **Summaries:** One paragraph per paper summarizing the main ideas and contributions.  
- **Synthesis:** Discussion of how the papers relate to each other and to the broader research field.  
- **References:** BibTeX-formatted references cited in the summaries.

The literature review focuses on recent advances in honeynet architectures, adaptive deception strategies, and their application to network and cyber-physical systems.
